Questions

Good to know

Is my money safe?

Yes. Your balance is held in a real, dedicated bank ledger through our regulated banking partner — not floating in an app. It’s store credit, spendable in-store, and always yours.

How do rewards work?

You earn loyalty points back on every purchase. Points are worth 1¢ each — 100 points equals $1.00 of store credit — and you can redeem them to your card instantly from the Rewards page.

What is auto top-up?

Auto top-up reloads your card automatically from your linked bank. Choose a low-balance trigger (e.g. when you drop below $50) or a weekly/monthly schedule. You can change the amount, pause, or cancel anytime.

How do I add money?

Link a bank account once with your routing and account number, then top up by ACH in seconds. Your bank details are sent securely to our banking partner and are never stored in the app.

Can I cash out my balance?

Shuk credit is closed-loop store credit, spendable in participating stores. It isn’t transferable to other people or cashable — that’s what keeps it secure and lets us reward you more than a debit card can.
